UP TOUR


Uttar Pradesh (UP), in northern India, is a culturally rich and historically significant state. It is home to the Taj Mahal in Agra, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a global symbol of love. Varanasi, one of the world's oldest cities, is a spiritual hub with its sacred ghats and the Ganga Aarti. The capital city, Lucknow, is known for its Nawabi heritage, Mughal architecture, and delectable Awadhi cuisine. Ayodhya and Mathura-Vrindavan are important religious sites linked to Lord Rama and Lord Krishna, respectively. The state hosts vibrant festivals like Holi, Diwali, and the Kumbh Mela, attracting millions of visitors. Wildlife enthusiasts can explore the Dudhwa National Park and Okhla Bird Sanctuary. Uttar Pradesh's diverse attractions, accessible by air, rail, and road, make it a must-visit destination. The best time to explore the state is from October to March..
