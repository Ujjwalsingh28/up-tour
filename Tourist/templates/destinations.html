Destinations

Varanasi

Varanasi – Ganga ghat

It is a city situated on the banks of Ganga river in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh dating to the 11th century B.C. Regarded as the spiritual capital of India.

Agra

Agra – City of Wonders

Agra, nestled on the banks of the Yamuna River, is famed for the iconic Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Fatehpur Sikri. A blend of Mughal history, rich culture, and vibrant markets, it remains a top tourist destination.

Vrindavan

Vrindavan – Sacred Town of Krishna

Vrindavan, known for its deep spiritual connection to Lord Krishna, is famous for its ancient temples and vibrant festivals. A revered destination for pilgrims worldwide.

Jaunpur

Jaunpur – Shahi Qila Jaunpur

Jaunpur is a city in southeastern Uttar Pradesh, India, situated approximately 55 kilometers northwest of Varanasi along the Gomati River. .

Ayodhya

Ayodhya – Birthplace of Lord Rama

Ayodhya, an ancient city on the banks of the Sarayu River, holds great religious significance as the birthplace of Lord Rama. Known for its temples and spiritual heritage, it is a major pilgrimage site for Hindus.

Lucknow

Lucknow – RUMI-DARWAZA LUCKNOW

Lucknow, known for its Nawabi culture, delectable cuisine, and elegant Chikankari embroidery, blends tradition with modernity.

Prayagraj

Prayagraj – Khusro Bagh

Prayagraj, at the confluence of the Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers, is renowned for the Kumbh Mela and its rich spiritual heritage.

Chitrakoot

Chitrakoot – Ram Ghat

Chitrakoot, a revered pilgrimage site, is known for its association with Lord Rama and its serene natural beauty, temples, and ghats.

Kanpur

Kanpur – JK TEMPLE IN KANPUR

Kanpur, a major industrial city in Uttar Pradesh, is known for its leather industry, educational institutions, and historical significance.

Etawah

Etawah – Safari Park

Etawah, located in Uttar Pradesh, is known for its historical landmarks, agricultural significance, and the Etawah Lion Safari. It serves as a regional center for trade and culture.

Mirzapur

Mirzapur – Wyndom Falls

Mirzapur is a city located in the northern part of Uttar Pradesh, India, known for its rich history, culture, and natural beauty. .

Hastinapur

Hastinapur – Bada Digambar Jain Mandir

Hastinapur is an ancient city in Uttar Pradesh, India, renowned for its historical and religious, where it served as the capital of the Kuru dynasty, home to the Pandavas and Kauravas.
