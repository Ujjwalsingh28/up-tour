Popular Faq's

The best time to visit Uttar Pradesh is between October and March when the weather is cool and pleasant. Summers (April to June) can be hot, while winters (November to February) offer a more comfortable climate for sightseeing.

  • By Air: Major airports in Uttar Pradesh are in Lucknow, Varanasi, and Agra.
  • By Train: Uttar Pradesh has a well-connected railway network, and cities like Agra, Lucknow, and Varanasi are major railway hubs.
  • By Road: The state is well connected via highways, and inter-state buses are available

Famous Cultural Experiences in Uttar Pradesh
1. Spirituality: Witness the Ganga Aarti in Varanasi and explore Ayodhya and Mathura-Vrindavan.
2. Festivals: Celebrate Holi in Vrindavan, Diwali in Ayodhya, and the Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj.
3. Cuisine: Savor Awadhi biryani, kebabs, and Banarasi paan.
4. Handicrafts: Discover Banarasi silk sarees, Chikankari embroidery, and Zardozi work.
5. Music and Dance: Enjoy Kathak dance and folk music traditions like Rasiya.

  • Agra: The Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, Fatehpur Sikri
  • Varanasi: Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Ghats of Varanasi, Sarnath
  • Lucknow: Bara Imambara, Chota Imambara, Rumi Darwaza
  • Mathura and Vrindavan: Temples related to Lord Krishna
  • Ayodhya: Ram Janmabhoomi, Hanuman Garhi

Research and plan your trip in advance based on the cities and places you want to visit. Hire a local guide for a more immersive experience, especially when visiting historical or religious sites. Consider booking tours that cover multiple cities, as travel distances can be significant..

  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Varanasi): Dedicated to Lord Shiva and one of the most sacred Hindu temples.
  • Sarnath (near Varanasi): A Buddhist pilgrimage site where Buddha delivered his first sermon.
  • Ram Janmabhoomi (Ayodhya): Believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama.
